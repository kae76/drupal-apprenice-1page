What is Drupal?


...and what is the Drupal Apprenticeship Scheme?

It's a two week bootcamp followed by a year long placement of hands-on learning in the workplace and additional training every month.You'll cover many aspects of web development from planning and prototyping through front and back end coding to deployment and delivery. You may start to specialise along the way but in one year you can become a full stack web developer using the Drupal framework.

At completion you will qualify with a level 4 industry gold apprenticeship qualification in Drupal Web Development - this is equivilent to a foundation degree and covers topics and areas chosen by industry leaders as the most important and valuable for you to get a head start in a career in web development.


Who is the scheme for?

The scheme is open to anyone aged 16 - 23 but we are specifically looking for people who do not have a university education but can display an aptitude and enthusiasm for web work.

Desired Skills

  • Ability to learn independently
  • Basic HTML and CSS
  • Able to work both alone and as part of a team
  • "Can-do" approach to workload

Personal qualities

  • Passionate about web development
  • Self-starter
  • Friendly - outgoing personality
  • Good timekeeping / reliability

Qualifications required

No qualifications are actually required by the scheme but employers prefer 5 A*-C GCSEs (or equivalents) including Maths, English and ICT
