What is the Drupal Apprenticeship Scheme?


You'll learn how to code and build websites from seasoned professionals and industry experts. You'll cover many aspects of web development and in one year you can become a full stack web developer using the Drupal framework.

At completion you will qualify with a level 4 industry gold apprenticeship qualification in Drupal Web Development - this is equivilent to a foundation degree and covers topics and areas chosen by industry leaders as the most important and valuable for you to get ahead in a career in web development.
